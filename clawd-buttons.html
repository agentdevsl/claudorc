Claw'd Button Variations

Hover over buttons to see Claw'd walk - animation triggers on hover only

Variation 1

Walk Across (2s)

Hover to see Claw'd walk from left to right across the button. Walks for 2 seconds and stops at the right edge.

Variation 2

Walk to Center

Hover to see Claw'd walk to the center and stop. Animation triggers on hover only.

Variation 3

Slow Walk (3s, larger)

Larger Claw'd with a slower, more leisurely walk. Hover to trigger - takes 3 seconds to cross.
